LikeThat Décor

LikeThat Décor is a tool to help you search for Décor with Images, not words, to quickly bring your decorating visions to life. When you find a piece of furniture you like you simply take a picture or use an photo off your phone to get instant access to similar looking pieces. It’s as simple as Snap it, Explore it, Find it. Learn more

 
 

PetMatch

The PetMatch app uses pictures to find adoptable pets in your area. Simply take a picture, upload from your collection, or use a picture from the PetMatch sample gallery to get started. Learn more



 
 


ShopScout

ShopScout allows you to find and discover products visually. With more than 200 million products available from 50,000+ online stores, users experience visual search with the most comprehensive shopping solution. Click on a product image and instantly see identical and similar product offers.


 
 
 

About Us


“ Our mission is to help people engage with the world around them”

 

Superfish is a pioneering visual search company. Through cutting-edge, patented technology we have developed a visual search engine that analyzes images algorithmically and transforms the way images are searched, seen, utilized and shared over the web. Quite Simply, we are opening up a new way to search.

Founded by Adi Pinhas and Michael Chertok, Superfish is headquartered in Palo Alto, California and is backed by Draper Fisher Jurvetson (DFJ) and Vintage Investment Partners.
